Compiling headers and message body
After each of these lines press enter to start the next line.
After the 3rd line, press enter twice so that it puts a blank line between the Subject, and the body of the email.
A period on a line all by itself indicates that is the end of the email.
It will then try to send this email to the recipient from the sender email address using the data you provided
in the headers above.
