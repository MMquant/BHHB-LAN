crunch
https://sourceforge.net/projects/crunch-wordlist/

wordlist generator


Examples

Use of Charsets

crunch 4 4 -f /usr/share/crunch/charset.lst mixalpha -o wordlist.txt

Generate 6 chars long passwords from ABCDEF123 and store to file

crunch 6 6 ABCDEF123 -o wordlist.txt

Generate custom passwords(see tag specifications in man page)

, Upper-ase Alpha
@ Lower-case alpha
^ Special char
% Number
# crunch 8 8 -t ,@@^^%%%
